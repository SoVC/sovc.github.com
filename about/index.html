About [SoVC]

Society of Villainous Chaps is a gaming clan for the decidedly underhanded traditional British gentleman. Our games of choice are:

  • EVE Online
  • Nuclear Dawn
  • Team Fortress 2

Naturally, we operate on a strict code of morality and ethics. Whilst we will be underhand and downright sneaky, we will do so in the finest gentlemanly way possible - terribly politely!

If you're interested in joining our quest to bring eloquence and dignity into the gaming realm, please have an inspection of the About subsection.
