Rank Structure

We employ a somewhat eclectic rank structure, however it uses many traditional style titles, which suits our style. Our rank structure are as follows (abbreviations in brackets), in descending order:

  1. Grand Master (Gra Mstr)
  2. Advisor General (Adv Gnrl)
  3. Advisor (Adv)
  4. Clan/Guild/Corporation Master (Cla/Gui/Cor Mstr)
  5. Knight General (Kni Gnrl)
  6. Knight Commander (Kni Cmdr)
  7. Knight (Kni)
  8. Adept (Ade)
  9. Acolyte (Aco)
  10. Initiate (Ini)
